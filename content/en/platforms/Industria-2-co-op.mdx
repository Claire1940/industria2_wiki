### 2) Tune your platform and network

If **Industria 2 co op** ships with online play, stability matters more than peak FPS. A smooth 90 FPS with low packet loss is better than unstable high FPS.

- Use wired Ethernet where possible  
- Enable game mode/QoS on your router  
- Close heavy background upload apps  
- Keep GPU and chipset drivers updated  
- Test your microphone gate/noise suppression in advance

> **Warning:** If co-op progression is host-dependent, disconnects can cause lost checkpoints for guests. Confirm save behavior before long sessions.

### 3) Align expectations on progression

Many co-op campaigns treat hosts and guests differently for story flags, unlocks, or achievement tracking. If **Industria 2 co op** uses partial progression sync, you may need to replay chapters so everyone stays aligned.

A simple “progression contract” for your group works well:

1. Main story only with the full squad  
2. Side content allowed solo  
3. No major story chapter starts without all core players  
4. Screenshot key objective completions after each session

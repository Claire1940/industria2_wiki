## Common Mistakes When Tracking Release Timing

If you keep Googling **Industria 2 when is coming out**, avoid these traps:

1. **Relying on reposted social snippets**  
   Aggregator accounts often repeat stale info without region context.

2. **Ignoring store-level timestamps**  
   “Released” and “Playable now” can differ by timezone and account.

3. **Assuming one platform equals all platforms**  
   A live PC launch does not confirm simultaneous console deployment.

4. **Confusing trailer upload time with unlock time**  
   The trailer can publish before store unlock by hours.

5. **Skipping technical prep**  
   Even if it’s live, outdated drivers or missing redistributables can make launch feel “unavailable.”

> **⚠️ Warning:** Don’t pre-judge launch quality from a single user report in the first few hours. Early data is noisy, especially for story-driven PC releases.
